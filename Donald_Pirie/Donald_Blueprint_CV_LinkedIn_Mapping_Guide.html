Your Career Blueprint contains everything you need to create a compelling CV and LinkedIn profile. This guide shows you exactly where each piece of insight should land.

üí° Pro Tips

  • CV vs LinkedIn: Your CV can be more formal; LinkedIn can be more conversational and personal
  • Tailor for roles: Use the Interactive CV Builder to swap in different headlines/statements for different opportunities
  • Keywords matter: Include industry terms (B2B, brand strategy, transformation) for searchability
  • Authenticity check: Read everything aloud. Does it sound like YOU would say it?
  • Non-negotiables stay hidden: Your green lights/red flags inform your choices but don't go on public documents
