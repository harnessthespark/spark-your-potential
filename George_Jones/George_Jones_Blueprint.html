Your Deal-Breakers Complete

Based on your patterns and what energises you vs drains you:

Red Flags to Avoid
Adding complexity to solve complexity: More tools, more layers, more abstraction
Document-driven culture: 6 months of planning before shipping
"Just make it work" pressure: No time to think strategically
Blame culture: Witch hunts instead of learning
No mentorship value: Senior ICs expected to just code
Green Flags to Pursue
Simplicity valued: "What can we remove?" is a valid question
Learning culture: Blameless postmortems, psychological safety
Mentorship expected: Growing engineers is part of the role
Influence without authority: Ideas win on merit, not seniority
Strategic thinking time: Space to see the bigger picture
