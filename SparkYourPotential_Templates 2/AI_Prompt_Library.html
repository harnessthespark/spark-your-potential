Story Structurer (STAR Method)
Structure your evidence into interview stories
Help me structure this achievement into a compelling interview story. The achievement: [ONE OF YOUR BELIEF MOMENTS OR KEY EVIDENCE] Structure it using STAR format: - Situation: What was the context? (2-3 sentences) - Task: What was I specifically asked to do? - Action: What did I actually do? (This should be the longest part) - Result: What happened? Include numbers if possible. Also give me: - A punchy opening line to hook the interviewer - The key "so what" ‚Äî why this matters - A bridge sentence to connect it to what I'm seeking now
