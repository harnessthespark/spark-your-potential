Week 1: Foundation Complete

Your Core Pattern

You don't fix strategies. You restore belief systems.

Across every transformation you've led—UBS, UPS, AMD—you turn confusion into conviction. Teams arrive stuck and paralysed; you find the human truth beneath the noise, and they don't just get a strategy—they get belief back.

This is belief transformation, not problem-solving.

Your Evidence Collection

Five transformations that reveal your unique pattern of impact:

Outcome #1: Effies Excellence

Helped 3 major agencies create more effective Effies papers, drawing a clear line between comms activities and business effects. Built stronger, tighter, more compelling stories that increased their chances of winning.

Outcome #2: UBS Digital Transformation

Enabled a digital brand journey, content magnets and campaign structure which increased both familiarity (brand team's goal) and leads (wealth management's goal), building new client relationships.

Elevated AMD from specs-driven competitor to visionary leader. Shifted from promoting specs to shaping progress. Platform: AMD Together We Advance. Impact: Global repositioning, multi-audience impact.

Company Ownership Insight

Key learning: Company ownership structure matters significantly.

Private companies (like Edelman) can be more strategic and less reactive to quarterly shareholder pressures. They may move slower, but they're often better environments for transformation work.

Holding company agencies often have a different ethos—more reactive, quarterly-focused, which can conflict with your need for depth and embedding.

Edelman – Freelance "Try Before You Buy" — Authenticity Score: 8/10

Why It Appeals: Chance to "build a bigger strategic offering over time." Edelman is expanding from PR into brand campaigns and B2B strategy. Private company structure = more strategic, less reactive.

Note: Things move slowly because they're private and undergoing reorganisation—but this is actually a positive sign for the kind of environment you need.

Interview Questions to Ask

About Transformation & Depth
  • "How does this role balance delivering quick wins vs embedding lasting transformation?"
  • "Is this a brand platform environment or more deck-and-done?"
  • "What's the typical timeline from strategy to implementation? Do strategists stay involved?"
About Culture & Authenticity
  • "What's valued more—having the loudest voice or the clearest thinking?"
  • "How does the company's ownership structure affect how decisions get made?"
  • "Tell me about someone who's thrived here. What made them successful?"
About Sustainability
  • "How does the team manage pace and workload?"
  • "Is marketing seen as a growth engine or a cost centre here?"
  • "What autonomy does this role have to manage approach and energy?"
