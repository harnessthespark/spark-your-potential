The Four Core Tests

Any opportunity should pass these tests to be worth your energy:

1. Pattern Fit

Does this role allow you to do what you do best?

  • Can I unlock creative potential here?
  • Is there a team or client that needs what I do?
  • Will I be connecting creativity with commercial reality?

2. Transformation Scope

Will you have the time and permission to create real change?

  • Can I stay until transformation is embedded?
  • Is this "execution only" or real change?
  • Will I have direct access to stakeholders?

3. Creative Ambition

Is there something worth unlocking?

  • Is this a stuck team that needs a catalyst?
  • Brand platform or "ad factory"?
  • Is there appetite for transformation?

4. Authentic Fit

Can you be yourself and thrive?

  • Will I have to mask to succeed here?
  • Does the culture value what I bring?
  • Can I sustain this long-term?
