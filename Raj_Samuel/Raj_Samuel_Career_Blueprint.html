Your Evidence Collection

Five career outcomes that reveal your unique pattern of impact:

AMV was systematically locked out of digital pitches. Brought in as freelance planning director for BT Digital pitch - we won. Then became Board Account Planner, Co-Innovation Director. Fallon brought me in for digital strategy. The drumming gorilla ad was insane - no chocolate, no bite. Client was terrified. Ran workshops to create belief, showed how content lives in the world now. Creative was brilliant - but it was the digital strategy and workshop that gave them courage to greenlight it.

Outcome #3: Arley's Brand Creation

First time client-side. Brand Director for plant-based startup. Transformed generic "plant-based startup" into "heritage comfort brand" - secured 3 major retailer listings in 12 months, ¬£1.2M pre-launch revenue. Cultural research revealed the "comfort paradox": 73% of flexitarian consumers wanted familiarity over innovation. Everyone chased "the future" when the opportunity was "the familiar, but better." Brand still going strong with their own factory, selling across Europe.

Outcome #4: Malice Arts - Immersive Events

2014-2019. Created immersive horror experiences - Malice in Wonderland, 1984 vs 1984, Inferno/Paradiso. Twisted pop culture: Tron crash-landing in a club, Narnia glitching inside the Matrix. Ten events, 5,000+ guests, 85% return rates, brand sponsorships, press coverage. Proved: when you create worlds people want to enter - not just events they attend - they come back. Audiences want to be transported, not just entertained.

Outcome #5: Lemons.life ‚Üí Quilter Partnership

Co-founded with Alex after her husband's death exposed broken financial planning for women. Created brand strategy - went with "Lemons" instead of narrow "wills" name. "Life will always throw Lemons." Spotted early that the real story wasn't about death - it was about women's financial vulnerability across career breaks, caring, divorce, widowhood. Quilter now investing. Women selling to women. Turning finance upside down for women.
