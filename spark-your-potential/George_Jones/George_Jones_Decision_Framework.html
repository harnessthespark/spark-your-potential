Key Questions to Ask

Ask these in every interview to uncover red flags and green flags:

1
What happened in your last major outage? Listen for blameless postmortems or finger-pointing. This reveals culture better than any values statement.
2
What's your on-call setup and rotation? Sustainable or always firefighting? Do people get properly compensated? This reveals operational maturity.
3
How do developers deploy to production? Self-service platform or tickets to ops? This reveals platform engineering investment.
4
What's your infrastructure automation story? Terraform everywhere or click-ops? This reveals technical maturity.
5
What happened to the last person in this role? Promoted, burned out, or left frustrated? This reveals the reality of the role.
