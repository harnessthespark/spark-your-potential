üìã CV Framework Structure

Your CV should follow this structure:

1. Header: Name, Title, Contact details
2. Profile: 3-4 sentences combining your Core Pattern + Thread + Evidence
3. Career Highlights: 4-5 quantified achievements (your best evidence)
4. Skills: Your core capabilities in comma-separated format
5. Sectors: Industries where you've proven impact
6. Career History: Roles with context ‚Üí action ‚Üí result bullets
7. Case Studies: 2-3 detailed examples showing your approach
8. Education & Awards: Credentials and recognition
