The Four Core Tests

Any opportunity should pass these tests to be worth your energy:

1. The Systems Thinking Test

Core Question: Will this role let me work across the full spectrum‚Äînot just one silo?

  • Can I connect brand strategy with digital solutions AND employee engagement AND customer reality?
  • Will I have visibility across content creation, innovation, and transformation?
  • Is this a "whole picture" role or am I being hired for just one narrow function?
  • Can I bridge marketing strategy with operational reality?
  • Will I have the autonomy to join the dots others miss?

If you're being siloed into just "brand" or just "digital" without seeing the connections, this fails the test.

2. The Innovation & Technology Test

Core Question: Will I be able to apply technology and innovation to solve real problems?

  • Is there appetite for AI, automation, and innovative solutions?
  • Will I be valued for bringing new approaches (like behavioural economics)?
  • Is "digital transformation" just a buzzword or genuinely embedded?
  • Can I help build and optimise, not just deliver what's always been done?
  • Is there budget and mandate for technology adoption?

Your 20.9% cost reduction through AI and ‚Ç¨484k savings at Siemens prove you can deliver‚Äîmake sure the org will let you.

3. The Impact & Measurement Test

Core Question: Will my contribution be measurable and visible?

  • Are there clear metrics for success (like your 368% at Lloyds)?
  • Will leadership see and value the results I create?
  • Is there a culture of measurement and accountability?
  • Can I point to outcomes, not just outputs?
  • Will I have case studies worth adding to my portfolio?

You've delivered Nike, JLR, Siemens-level work. Don't accept roles where your impact gets lost in noise.

4. The Variety & Sector Fit Test

Core Question: Will this role give me the breadth I need across clients/challenges?

  • Will I work across multiple sectors (Financial Services, Pharma, Automotive, FMCG)?
  • Is there variety in the problems I'll solve?
  • Does the client/portfolio include organisations I respect?
  • Will I learn something new, or is it just repeating what I've done?
  • Is there potential for Save the Children-style purpose-driven work alongside commercial?

Your range is your superpower. Roles that narrow you to just one sector may not energise you.
